About The Looong Weekend

Our Story

The Looong Weekend was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to help people make the most of their weekends and transform short breaks into extraordinary adventures.

Since 2020, we've been curating exceptional travel experiences that fit perfectly into busy schedules. We understand that not everyone can take extended vacations, but that shouldn't stop you from exploring the world.

Our team of travel experts carefully selects destinations and crafts itineraries that maximize your time while ensuring quality, comfort, and unforgettable memories.
