# Instagram Content Import Guide

This guide will help you import photos and content from your Instagram page into the website.

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Download Instagram Photos

1. **Visit your Instagram**: https://www.instagram.com/the.looong.weekend/
2. **For each post you want to use**:
   - Click on the post to open it
   - Right-click on the image → "Save Image As..."
   - Save with a descriptive name (e.g., `kerala-tour-1.jpg`, `beach-sunset.jpg`)

### Step 2: Organize Photos

Place photos in the appropriate folders:

```
assets/images/
├── tours/          # Photos that show tours/experiences
├── destinations/    # Photos of locations/destinations  
├── gallery/         # General gallery photos
└── testimonials/    # Customer photos (if any)
```

### Step 3: Document Content

For each photo, note:
- **Caption** from Instagram
- **Location/Tour name** mentioned
- **Category** (Beach, Adventure, Cultural, etc.)
- **Highlights** or activities shown

### Step 4: Update Website Content

See instructions below for updating tours, destinations, and gallery.
